<h2>Your Pre-Consultation &amp; Ongoing Checklist:</h2>
<p><strong>Phase 1: Before You Engage (Choosing the Right Consultant)</strong></p>
<ol>
<li><strong>What is your specific experience with projects like mine?</strong> (e.g., residential, commercial, specific geographical area, type of flood risk – fluvial, pluvial, groundwater).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Ensures they have relevant, practical experience. <a href="https://aegaea.com/case-studies/">Aegaea&#39;s case studies</a> showcase our diverse experience.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are your qualifications and accreditations?</strong> (e.g., CIWEM, ICE, relevant degrees).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Verifies their professional standing and expertise.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Can you provide references or testimonials from previous clients?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Offers insight into their service quality and client satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What is your typical process for a Flood Risk Assessment (FRA) or the specific service I need?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Helps you understand their methodology and what to expect. Learn about the <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/beyond-the-maps-site-visit-crucial-for-fra">importance of site visits</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are your estimated fees and what exactly is included (and excluded)?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Ensures transparency and helps you budget. (See our article on the <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/real-world-savings-flood-risk-due-diligence">real-world savings of due diligence</a>).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What is your current workload and estimated turnaround time for my project?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Manages expectations regarding project timelines.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>How do you approach communication and updates throughout the project?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Good communication is key to a successful partnership. <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/human-touch-specialist-consultants-demystify-flood-data">Aegaea prioritises clear communication</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Phase 2: During the Assessment / Project</strong></p>
<ol start="8">
<li><strong>What specific flood data sources are you using for my site?</strong> (e.g., Environment Agency maps, LiDAR, historical records, local authority data like SFRA).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Understands the basis of their assessment.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are the primary flood risks identified for my property/site, and what are their probabilities/return periods?</strong> (e.g., 1 in 100 year fluvial, 1 in 30 year surface water).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Clarifies the nature and severity of the risks. (For an explanation of <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/flood-zone-x-what-does-it-mean">Flood Zone X/1, read this</a>).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Can you explain what these risks practically mean for my development/property?</strong> (e.g., potential flood depths, impact on access, safety, building fabric).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Translates technical data into real-world implications.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are the proposed mitigation or resilience measures, and why are these the most appropriate?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Ensures you understand the solutions and their rationale. Explore <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/suds-guide-homeowners-small-developers">SuDS for homeowners</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are the estimated costs and maintenance requirements for these measures?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Considers the long-term financial implications.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>How will these measures impact the usability/design/value of my property/development?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Balances flood safety with other project goals.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Are there any residual risks I should be aware of even after mitigation?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Provides a complete picture of ongoing risk.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>How will climate change allowances impact the assessment and recommendations?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Ensures future-proofing. (Learn about <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/climate-change-allowances-fra-guide">climate change in FRAs</a>).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>What are the next steps once the report is complete?</strong> (e.g., submission to LPA, discussions with insurers).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Clarifies the onward process. This can be vital if <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/planning-permission-denied-flood-risk-expert-help">planning permission has been refused</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Phase 3: Reviewing the Report/Deliverables</strong></p>
<ol start="17">
<li><strong>Can you walk me through the key findings and recommendations in plain English?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Ensures you fully understand the report&#39;s content.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Are there any specific limitations to this assessment I should be aware of?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Understands the scope and boundaries of the work.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>If I have follow-up questions after reading the report, are you available to discuss them?</strong><ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Confirms ongoing support.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>How can this report be used to support planning applications, insurance discussions, or property transactions?</strong> (Relevant to your specific need, for instance, <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/insurance-nightmares-proper-fra-can-save-hassle">navigating insurance nightmares</a>).<ul>
<li><em>Why ask?</em> Maximises the utility of the report.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Why Asking Questions Matters</strong></p>
<p>Asking informed questions not only helps you understand the technicalities but also builds a better working relationship with your consultant. It demonstrates your engagement and helps them tailor their advice more effectively. An expert consultant from an <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/smb-advantage-bespoke-flood-risk-solutions">SMB like Aegaea</a> will welcome your questions and see them as an opportunity to provide greater clarity and value.</p>
<p>Remember, there&#39;s no such thing as a silly question when it comes to protecting your property and investments from flood risk. The <a href="https://camerhann.github.io/blog/true-cost-of-ignoring-flood-risk">true cost of <em>not</em> understanding</a> can be far higher than the time taken to ask.</p>
